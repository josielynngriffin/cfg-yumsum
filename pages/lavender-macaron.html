LAVENDER MACARONS

PREP TIME: 60 minutes | COOK TIME: 15 minutes | TOTAL TIME: 1 hour 15 minutes

This delicious homemade recipe is the go- to recipe this Christmas. This recipe is delicous and comforting. It's easy to make from scratch in your kitchen with easy to find items in your kitchen.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 eggs whites
  • 1 cup finely ground almond flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1½ cups confectioners' sugar
  • 2 drops lavender essential oil
  • 1 drop purple gel food coloring (optional!)

DIRECTIONS

Step 1

Line a baking sheet with silicone baking mat.


Step 2

Beat egg whites in the bown of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment until foamy. Add white sugar, food coloring, and lavender essential oil. Then beat until egg whites are glossy, fluffy, and hold soft peaks.


Step 3

Shift confectioners sugar and ground almonds in a seperate bowl; quickly fold almonds mixture into egg whites, about 30 strokes.


Step 4

Spoon a small amount of batter into a plastic bag with a small corner cut off and pipe a test disk of batter, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter, onto the prepared baking sheet. If the disk of batter holds a peak instead of flattening immediately, gently fold batter a few more times and retest.


Step 5

When batter is mixed enough to flatten immediately into an even disk, spoon into a pastry bag fitted with a plain round tip. Pipe batter onto the baking sheet in rounds, leaving space between the disks. Let piped cookies stand out at room temperature until they form a hard skin on top, about 1 hour.


Step 6

Preheat the oven to 285 degrees F (140 degrees C).


Step 7

Bake cookies in preheated oven until set but not browned, about 10 minutes.

Step 8

Let cookies cool completely before filling, about 30 minutes.
