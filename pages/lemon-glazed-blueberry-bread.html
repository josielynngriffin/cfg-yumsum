LEMON GLAZED BLUEBERRY BREAD

PREP TIME: 15 minutes | COOK TIME: 1 hour | TOTAL TIME: 1 hour 15 minutes

INGREDIENTS

    1¾ cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • &frac18 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sour cream, room temperature
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 TBSP lemon juice
  • 2 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 cups (about 1 pint) fresh or frozen blueberries, divided
  • 1 cup (126 gr) powdered, confectioners, or icing sugar
  • 2 tsp lemon zest
  • 2-4 TBSP (30-59 ml) lemon juice

DIRECTIONS

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350ºF. Prepare a 9x5" loaf pan with parchment paper or lightly oil and set aside.

Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.

Step 3

In a separate bowl, lightly whisk together the eggs, oil, vanilla, sour cream, and lemon juice and zest.

Step 4

Pour the liquid ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir together until just combined. The batter will be pretty thick. Do not over-mix. Fold about 1½ cups of the blueberries into the batter.

Step 5

Pour the batter into the loaf pan and sprinkle the remaining blueberries over the top.

Step 6

Bake at 350 for about 55-70 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean from the middle.

Step 7

Cool completely on a cooling rack before removing from the pan and glazing. For the glaze, mix powdered sugar, lemon zest, and enough lemon juice that the mixture can be poured. Drizzle over loaf.
